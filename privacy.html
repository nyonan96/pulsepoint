Privacy Policy

Last Updated: March 1, 2026

1. Information Collection

Pulse collects information to provide a seamless health tracking experience:

  • Account Data: Email and UID via Firebase Authentication.
  • Health Logs: Mood entries, battery levels, and memos stored in Cloud Firestore.
  • Medical Data: Medication names, dosages, and appointment schedules.

2. Data Usage

Your data is used solely to generate "Mental Forecasts," sync information across your devices, and send local/push notifications for reminders.

3. Third-Party Services

We utilize Google Firebase for secure data storage and Expo for notification delivery. We do not sell or share your personal health logs with advertisers.

4. Data Control

You may delete your data at any time through the app interface. For permanent account deletion, please contact support at curvstudios96@gmail.com.
